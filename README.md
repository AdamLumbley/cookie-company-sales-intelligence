## Overview

This project is a Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance for a fictional B2B cookie company. It transforms raw sales data into interactive visual insights covering revenue, volume, client performance, time trends, and geographic distribution.

The goal is to simulate a real-world business intelligence workflow using Power BI.

---

## Objectives

This dashboard was built to answer the following business questions:

- Which clients generate the most revenue?
- How does sales volume compare across clients?
- What is the average order value by client?
- How does revenue trend over time?
- Where is revenue geographically concentrated?

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- ArcGIS Maps (Power BI integration)  
- Excel (data source)

---

## Dashboard Structure

### Row 1: Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Revenue  
- Total Units Sold  
- Average Order Value (AOV)

This section provides a high-level summary of business performance.

---

### Row 2: Time Series Analysis
- Revenue trend over a 3-year period  
- Interactive time slicer  

This section highlights growth patterns and trends over time.

---

### Row 3: Client & Geographic Analysis
- Revenue by Client  
- Units Sold by Client  
- Revenue by Location (ArcGIS Map)

This section identifies key revenue drivers and geographic concentration.

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue is concentrated among a small number of key clients  
- Revenue and volume are strongly correlated across clients  
- A small subset of clients drives most of the business  
- Sales trends show variation across time periods  
- Geographic data reveals regional revenue clustering
